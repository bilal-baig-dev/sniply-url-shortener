# Privacy Policy

At Sniply, your privacy matters to us. Here's a quick overview of how we handle your data:

1. **Data Collection**:  
   We collect minimal data such as your email, IP address, and usage patterns to improve our service.

2. **Cookies**:  
   We use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering preferences and tracking analytics.

3. **Third-Party Services**:  
   Some third-party services (e.g., analytics or email providers) may collect data to support our operations.

4. **Data Usage**:  
   Your data will never be sold. It is used only to improve, operate, and communicate with you about Sniply.

5. **User Rights**:  
   You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting [support@sniply.com](mailto:support@sniply.com).

6. **Policy Updates**:  
   We may update this policy. Continued use of Sniply means you accept any changes.
